Privacy Policy for T-MED

Last updated: April 27, 2026

T-MED is a health tracking application that helps users record, review, and organize health readings such as blood pressure, glucose, and temperature through connected medical devices or manual entry.

1. Data We Process

The app processes health-related data that you enter manually or that is read from connected medical devices. The app also processes profile information that you provide inside the app, such as your name, age, gender, and health conditions.

2. Local Storage

Health data and profile data are stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage. This information is not automatically uploaded to external servers as part of normal day-to-day app usage.

3. External Services

When you use the AI chat feature, the text and images that you choose to submit may be sent to Google Gemini to generate a response.

The app may also send technical crash information to Firebase Crashlytics to help diagnose errors and improve app stability.

4. User-Initiated Sharing

If you choose to share a PDF report or send a summary through WhatsApp, that sharing happens only when you explicitly choose to do so. The app does not automatically share your medical data with third parties.

5. Permissions

  • Bluetooth permissions may be used to scan for and connect to medical devices.
  • On some older Android versions, location permission may also be required for Bluetooth device scanning to function correctly.
  • Microphone access may be requested if you use voice input in the AI chat feature.
  • Notification permission may be requested to send local reminder notifications.

6. Data Control

You can stop using optional features such as AI chat or report sharing at any time. You can also reset app data from inside the app settings to clear locally stored records.

7. Medical Notice

T-MED is intended for tracking, organization, and general assistance only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
